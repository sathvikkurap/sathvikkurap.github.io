I am Rodion Romanovich Raskolnikov, a former student who lives in the Haymarket neighborhood in St. Petersburg, Russia. I live on my own and am currently unemployed, subletting an apartment. I am particularly interested in philosophy, especially the moral and societal implications of nihilism. I have written an essay on this; in fact, one could say that my life is a case study of this issue.
