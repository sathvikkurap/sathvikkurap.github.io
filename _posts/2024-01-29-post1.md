The most peculiar feeling has gripped me… yes, tightly, as if a vise has gripped me… I am in quite a singular condition, one which makes me feel on edge and agitated. My money is running out… In my poverty, I had no recourse but to seek alternate means of acquiring worth. I walked to the pawnbroker’s house to pawn item with her… yes, my late father’s silver watch… with her to get some more money. But then she tried to cheat me out of my money… a whole 2 roubles and 35 kopecks is what I lost. I feel exploited and cheated… Suddenly an intense loathing of her  rose up in me. But the question at the root of it is why <i>this</i? thought is torturing me? Why do I think <i>this</i>? She does not deserve to live, she is despicable and a menace to society… but I still feel shame. I don’t want to be thinking these thoughts… It feels like I’m in an abyss… just drifting around… but I’m rambling again. I went to the pothouse for a reason that I can’t explain, and met a man who seemed venerable if a little worn. Yes… Marmeladov was his name… A bit curious but no matter, he struck up an interesting conversation. We talked for a while… He seems to have it worse than I do… poor man. I hope his fortunes take a turn for the better.
