I can feel my desires coming up… They seize me, and I cannot control myself... why have I lost control... That dream was confusing… poor horse. But I can’t be pitying the victim; after all, <i> I may soon become the oppressor myself!</i> The idea is broiling up inside of me... I can feel it slowly growing in power. But what a strange coincidence… that I saw Lizaveta… and learned an exact time when Alyona would be alone… to do <i>that</i>. Will I really… no, I cannot even bear to think of it, it disgusts me. But at the same time, my thoughts swirl around me like a blizzard, and I hear them howling… I have no refuge, because the thoughts are inside of me. It feels like I am doomed to my fate.
But really, what a laughable supposition! Surely I couldn’t hope to muster the courage to do it! Yes, I even lost courage before, and I was merely examining the area then! Yes, I would never do <i>that</i>! It’s all nonsense, it is! Just nonsense…
