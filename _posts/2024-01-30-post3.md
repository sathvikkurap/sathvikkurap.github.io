I'm shivering...shivering...it's so cold. I just managed to escape--narrowly, but unscathed nonetheless. It doesn't seem like nonsense anymore...not when everything's covered in red. Red! My clothes are red! Why is everything red? I’ve scoured myself for traces… No, no, no, it cannot be. I couldn’t have. Of course, I am in delirium… They must already know. They will know the moment they see me…see my condition. The way I have wretchedly acted, yes, they will know immediately! Yet, was I wrong? Am I pathetic for granting so many this relief? This revenge? Pah…how could I think such despicable, horrid things? There is no salvation…I must say something. Say something!
